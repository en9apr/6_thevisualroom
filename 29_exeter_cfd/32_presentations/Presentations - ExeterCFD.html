These are (some of) the presentations I have made at various conferences etc which have not been included elsewhere in the Wiki. No particular order (I'd like to keep them in date order but can't guarantee even that)


  • Training slides on programming in OpenFOAM as used at the 10th OpenFOAM Workshop in Ann Arbor, 2015. This is a modified version of the ones used at the 1st UKRI OpenFOAM Users Group meeting in Bristol
  • 2nd UK&RI Users Group meeting training slides on Multiphase flow
  • Hamburg (Open Source CFD conference) presentation on turbulence modelling with GA's
  • Presentation on the origins and capabilities of OpenFOAM for a meeting at Loughborough
  • Presentation for Exeter HPC meeting to discuss replacement computer for Zen
  • Invited presentation at Daresbury STFC lab - 2014
  • Two versions of a presentation about the group; from 2012 and a slightly more recent version delivered to CFMS in 2014
  • Presentation on CFD delivered at U. Florence (and again at the Politecnico di Milano)
  • Image-based Meshing; OpenFOAM Export for ScanIP -- presentation at the OpenFOAM Conference 2011 and associated conference paper
  • ECCOMAS 2006 conference paper on patient-specific arterial flow simulation
